📋 Championship Rules
Task Distance Duration (TDD) · AIR³ Paragliding
⚖️ General Rules
1
All standard airspace regulations apply at all times.
2
The pilot must pass through the Start (SSS) before attempting any turnpoints.
3
Turnpoints (TP) can be reached in any order after the start. The Goal must be reached last.
4
The flight window closes at 6:00 PM. Pilots must land at or before Goal by 6 PM. Tracking must stop at 6 PM.
🏆 Scoring — Each Task is worth 1000 points
100
pts per Turnpoint
200
pts for Goal
500
pts Distance (XContest)
5
Distance points are calculated as a proportion of 500 pts based on the furthest completed task flown that day. The pilot with the highest XContest score receives 500 pts. All others receive a proportional score:

Your Distance Points = (Your XContest Score ÷ Best XContest Score) × 500
6
Distance is measured as per XContest points for the flight.
⚠️ Important — Flight Submission Rules

Flights must NOT be submitted if any of the following apply:
  • The flight does not comply with airspace regulations
  • The tracklog continues after reaching Goal
  • The tracklog continues after 6:00 PM

Only the organiser can make a decision to validate a tracklog under specific conditions. Contact the organiser if you are unsure whether your track can be submitted.
🤝 Spirit of the Championship

This championship is based on honesty. Any pilot participating in the championship can protest against the validity of another pilot's flight if the rules are not respected. All protests should be directed to the organiser.
